Our Mission Statement

    Have you ever dreamed of a tech-help website with:

    • Simple, easy to read responses to common coding problems?
    • Friendlier, less pedantic programmers who don't just answer every question with "Google It"?
    • A question catalog laser-focused on providing insight to the most current coding subjects?

    Well, if so...then I'm afraid this website isn't for you.

    Here at The "Help" Desk, we take the quotations in our name very seriously:

    • You WON'T receive much genuine help here...But after using our platform you will technically be seeking "help" from a therapist.
    • You WILL encounter deeply confused users who ask non-programmatic questions on a supposedly programming-related site.
    • You WILL encounter developers so distraught by the silly questions they're receiving that they are even more hostile than on StackOverflow!

    Feel free to browse through our catalog of exceedingly random posts....

    and exceedingly exasperated answers from underpaid devs.
